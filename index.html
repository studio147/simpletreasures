Our Story

A unique vendor thrift experience

Welcome to Simple Treasures! We aren't your average thrift store. We operate as a vendor-based shop, meaning we host a variety of local sellers under one roof.

This unique model allows us to offer an incredible, constantly changing mix of brand new items alongside beloved used pieces. Whether you are hunting for vintage decor, everyday essentials, or handmade local crafts, you'll find it here.

Located right in Wagener, SC, our goal is to support local vendors while providing our community with a fun, affordable, and welcoming place to shop, laugh, and chat!
